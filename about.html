Nitya Buch

Hello, I am a Graphic & Motion Designer based in Toronto, Ontario. With education in the field of Interactive Media Design, I am currently exploring the world of 3D modeling.


I am a conscious Vegan and I strongly believe that morality and empathy are one of the most important virtues of a human being. I love mathematics and logical thinking, which are the base for most of my design work.


My interests revolve around films, Hindi music, live sports and non-fiction books. Calvin & Hobbes by Bill Waterson has taught me more about life than any philosophical literature out there.
