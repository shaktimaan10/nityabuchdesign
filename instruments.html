Revisiting the 70's

NOISE CHANNEL

The Marble pattern on the material is created by adding a fractal noise in the alpha channel of a white C4D material. Adding a hue to the Alpha layered material gives the material the desired pattern of natural marble lines for all the instrument casing.

telephone

Inspired by the early design of the telephone, the rotary handset has a microphone and a speaker connected with a wiring to the circuit mounted on a table top.

The reciever is gold plated with the handle made out of marble, rested on a metal stand.

the dial is designed with transparent marble glass material for the number buttons.

telephone

There is a wrist watch dial attached to the telephone while the electric wiring is nested in a circuit box under the wooden block.

The bells,dials, rotating wheel add to the detailing of the model and also are essential to making it look aesthetic.

The curling wire connected to the reciever is also characteristic of old telephone mechanism, which is a now rarity.

typewriter

A machine with relatively more details, the typewriter has a box encasing the mechanism.

The keys are mounted on a box stand, each one connected by a lever to the ink printer which ultimately prints on the paper manifold.
